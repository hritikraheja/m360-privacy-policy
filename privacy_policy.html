Muslim 360 Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3rd Jan 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile application (“Muslim 360”).

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited information only to provide accurate prayer timings and to improve app stability.

a. Location Information

  • The App may access your device’s location (GPS) to calculate accurate prayer times based on your geographic area.
  • Location access is optional.
  • If you do not allow location access, you can manually search and select your city.
  • We do not store or retain precise GPS coordinates.

b. City Information

  • When you allow location access or manually select a city, the city name may be used for prayer time calculation.
  • City name data may be logged anonymously for analytics purposes to understand usage patterns.

c. App Usage & Diagnostics

We use Firebase services to collect:

  • Anonymous app usage statistics (Firebase Analytics)
  • Crash reports and performance diagnostics (Firebase Crashlytics)

This data helps us improve app reliability and performance.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information only to:

  • Calculate accurate prayer timings
  • Understand general app usage trends
  • Identify and fix app crashes or technical issues

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data. Limited anonymous data is processed by Google Firebase (Analytics and Crashlytics) solely for app analytics and diagnostics, in accordance with Google’s privacy policies.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • We do not create user accounts.
  • We do not store personally identifiable information.
  • All data transmitted is encrypted in transit using industry-standard security measures.

5. User Choices

  • You may deny location permission and manually select your city.
  • You may disable analytics collection through your device settings where supported.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
